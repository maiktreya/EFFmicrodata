
Managers: Occupations that involve planning, organizing, directing, and controlling the activities of others.
Professionals: Occupations that require advanced knowledge and skills in a particular field, typically acquired through formal education and training.
Technicians and Associate Professionals: Occupations that require technical knowledge and skills in a particular field, typically acquired through specialized training or on-the-job experience.
Clerical Support Workers: Occupations that involve the performance of administrative and secretarial tasks, typically requiring a good level of literacy and numeracy.
Service and Sales Workers: Occupations that involve the provision of services to customers or the sale of goods.
Skilled Agricultural, Forestry and Fishery Workers: Occupations that require specialized knowledge and skills in the agricultural, forestry, or fishing industries.
Craft and Related Trades Workers: Occupations that require the manual skills to make, repair, or maintain goods and structures.
Plant and Machine Operators, and Assemblers: Occupations that involve the operation of machinery and equipment, or the assembly of goods.
Elementary Occupations: Occupations that involve routine tasks that require little or no specialized knowledge or skills.
Armed Forces Occupations: Occupations that involve the defense of a country's territory and people.
